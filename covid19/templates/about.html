About Us

In these dreadful COVID19 pandemic times, the world and its people are suffering and the doctors along with the other healthcare works like nurses are putting all their efforts and time into treating people. So we have come up as an endeavor to assist the people further by providing them 3 A's Availability, Accessibility and Affordability through our products. Using our webapp people will be able to find beds, ventilators, oxygen cylinders and medicines etc directly from the suppliers. Our main aim is to improve access for the common people to these necessities and assist them as much as we can.
